Thanks to Lemon Project ([http://www.wm.edu/sites/lemonproject/](http://www.wm.edu/sites/lemonproject/) ) Director and History Professor Jody Allen for agreeing to host the next meeting of Universities Studying Slavery (USS). The next meeting will take place in Williamsburg, Virginia, on September 30 and October 1, 2016.
Please stay tuned for meeting details. The consortium has a current membership of eighteen different institutions.
If you or your institution are interested in attending or considering joining the consortium, please contact President's Commission on Slavery and the University Co-Chair Kirt von Daacke at [kv2h@virginia.edu](mailto:kv2h@virginia.edu).
