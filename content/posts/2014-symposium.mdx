The President's Commission on Slavery and the University also hosted the symposium *Universities Confronting the Legacy of Slavery* on October 16th–17th, 2014, in Charlottesville, Virginia.

All events (excluding the keynote address) were [livestreamed on our home page](/index.html). The livestream was additionally broadcast at the Jefferson School African American Heritage Center.
